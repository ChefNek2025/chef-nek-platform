About Chef Nek

I built this because I lived this problem

"I walk into restaurants across the DMV and see the same thing — incredible food, talented cooks, but no system. Everything's in someone's head. That's not a business, that's a risk."

Chef Nek has been cooking professionally for over 15 years. He's watched great restaurants struggle not because the food wasn't good, but because they had no recipes written down, no prep system, no way to train new staff, no idea what their food cost was.

His TikTok blew up because people recognized the truth in what he was saying. Now he's turning that energy into a real platform — built for the DMV, built for restaurants that cook the real way.
